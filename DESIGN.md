# Workshift — Design System (Rendani)

> Source of truth for visual identity, components, and animations for the Workshift landing page.

## 1. Core Principles
- **Minimalism**: Clear hierarchy, ample white space.
- **Premium Motion**: Smooth, organic, GPU-accelerated animations.
- **Technical Precision**: Monospaced accents to emphasize data-driven results.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Sage (`#E6E8DD`) for a professional, calm feel.
- **Accent**: Lime (`#9CE069`) for CTA and high-priority highlights.
- **Ink**: Dark (`#000000`) for text and structural components.
- **Muted**: Slate tones for secondary descriptions.

## 3. Animation Standards

### Floating (New)
Subtle ambient motion applied to decorative and low-hierarchy elements.
- **Method**: `translateY` and `rotate` loops.
- **Curve**: `easeInOutSine`.
- **Duration**: 4s - 12s (varied to avoid robotic synchronization).

### Reveals
- **Text Reveal**: Word-by-word with blur reveal.
- **Fade Up**: Standard entry for sections (30px offset).

## 4. Typography
- **Headings**: Inter (Normal/Bold), tight tracking.
- **Accents**: IBM Plex Mono for sequence numbers and labels.
- **Body**: Inter for readability.
